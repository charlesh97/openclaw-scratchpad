# Summary: Unlocking the Forecasting Economy (arXiv:2604.20421)

**Authors:** Huaiyu Jia et al.
**Published:** April 22, 2026
**Dataset URL:** https://www.polymonitor.club/

## Key Details

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Markets | 770K+ |
| Fills | 943M+ |
| Oracle Events | ~2M |
| Date Range | Oct 2020 – Mar 2026 |
| Update Cadence | Continuously maintained |

## TL;DR

Largest publicly available prediction market dataset. Covers every aspect of the market lifecycle — creation, trading, and resolution. 943 million individual trades over 5.5 years of Polymarket history.

## Relevance to Our Bot

- Essential for backtesting: can simulate any arb strategy on 5+ years of real data
- Fill-level granularity: can model execution costs (slippage, fees)
- Cross-reference with Kalshi: the paper's methodological framework for combining datasets points the way for cross-platform arb analysis
