# Awesome Prediction Market Tools

**Source:** https://github.com/aarora4/Awesome-Prediction-Market-Tools

## What it does
A curated list of prediction market tools — AI Agents, Analytics, APIs, Dashboards, Copy Trading, Alerting, Tracking and More. The most comprehensive directory of prediction market tools discovered.

## Key Listings Relevant to Our Work
- **Eventarb** — Free tool for calculating cross-platform arbitrage opportunities (Polymarket, Kalshi, Robinhood)
- **Polytrage** — Real-time Polymarket arbitrage alert service via Telegram, delivering automated signals every 15 minutes with bid-ask spreads and profit calculations
- **PolyInsider** — Whale trade tracking and insider activity detection
- **Cogito** — AI agent for prediction market trading
- **Polymarket Elon Tracker** — Specialized Telegram bot for Elon Musk tweet count markets

## Why it matters
Gives us a map of the competitive landscape. Shows what tools already exist, where gaps are, and what we could build that's differentiated.
