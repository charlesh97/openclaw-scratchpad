# Paper Summary: Arbitrage Analysis in Polymarket NBA Markets

**arXiv:** 2605.00864  
**Authors:** Guang Cheng, Jiaxin Yang et al.  
**Date:** April 22, 2026  

## Research Question
Can arbitrage opportunities be profitably executed in Polymarket NBA prediction markets?

## Methodology
- Moneyline and Spread predictions for NBA games on Polymarket
- Minimum liquidity threshold: $10 USDC
- Time-series analysis of cross-market pricing

## Key Results
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Moneyline-Spread arb episodes | 290 |
| Average executable size | 14.8 shares |
| Constrained by liquidity | 76.9% |
| Key concentration | Final minutes of live play |

## Practical Takeaways
1. Sports arb exists but at retail scale only
2. Need real-time game data feeds to detect opportunities during play
3. Opportunities cluster around abrupt scoring events
4. Not viable for meaningful capital deployment

## Relevance to Our Research: Low-Medium
Good to know as a reference but not actionable at meaningful scale.
