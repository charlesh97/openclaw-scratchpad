# Summary: Unravelling the Probabilistic Forest

**Authors:** Saguillo, O., Ghafouri, V., Kiffer, L., & Suarez-Tangil, G.  
**Published:** August 2025 (IMDEA Networks Institute)  
**Venue:** AFT 2025 (LIPIcs)  
**arXiv:** 2508.03474

The landmark paper for prediction market arbitrage. Key contribution: two-type taxonomy of arbitrage (market rebalancing vs combinatorial) with empirical validation showing $40M+ in realized profits. The 40% median market deviation ($0.60) proves these markets are far from efficient.
