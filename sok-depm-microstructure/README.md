# SoK: Market Microstructure for Decentralized Prediction Markets (DePMs)

**Source:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.15612

## Summary

A comprehensive Systematization of Knowledge paper on decentralized prediction market microstructure. Published October 2025. Frames the open questions across prediction market venues, providing a methodological taxonomy of market types, pricing mechanisms, and arbitrage strategies.

## Key contributions

1. **Taxonomy of DePM market types:** CLOB-based (Polymarket), AMM-based (Gnosis), hybrid
2. **Microstructure analysis framework:** Methodological domain for analyzing order book dynamics
3. **Open research questions:** Identifies gaps in understanding of wash trading, market manipulation, and information aggregation
4. **Cross-venue comparison:** How different market designs affect price discovery

## Why it matters for bot building

Provides the theoretical foundation for understanding *why* arbitrage opportunities exist in prediction markets. Essential reading before designing a bot — helps identify which structural inefficiencies are exploitable.

## Implementability: 2/5

Research paper, not code. Provides theory and taxonomy invaluable for strategy design but no executable code.
