# Summary: The Anatomy of a Decentralized Prediction Market

**Authors:** Multiple (preprint)
**Published:** April 2026
**arXiv:** 2604.24366

## TL;DR
Largest-ever empirical study of Polymarket's order book microstructure using 30B+ events. Eight stylized facts that any trading bot must account for.

## Key Metrics
- 30 billion events over 52 days
- 600 pre-registered market panel
- Sub-50ms median data ingestion delay
- Wash trading at 1% median, 22% upper tail

## Relevance to Vega Research
Critical infrastructure research. The category-conditional spread findings directly impact which markets we target for arb. Sports markets have different microstructure than crypto markets — our bot needs category-specific parameters.
