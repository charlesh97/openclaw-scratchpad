# Anatomy of a Decentralized Prediction Market — TL;DR

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Title** | The Anatomy of a Decentralized Prediction Market: Microstructure Evidence from the Polymarket Order Book |
| **Date** | April 2026 |
| **Link** | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24366 |

## In one sentence

30 billion order book events analyzed to reveal Polymarket's unique microstructure — confirming that arbitrage exists but is structurally bounded by liquidity.

## Key data points for bot builders

| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| Median wash trade 1% (tail 22%) | Volume isn't reliable signal |
| Longshot spread premium | Favor moderate probability markets |
| Category spreads differ | Sports ≠ Crypto ≠ Politics |
| Sub-50ms ingestion lag | Need dedicated infrastructure for edge |
| Liquidity is shallow | Arb opportunities are retail-scale only |
