# Summary: SoK — Market Microstructure for DePMs

**arXiv:2510.15612** | Oct 2025 (v3 Mar 2026)

## One-Page TL;DR

A comprehensive **Systematization of Knowledge** paper covering the history of decentralized prediction markets from 2011 to present. Reviews hundreds of proposals and presents an **8-stage modular workflow**:

1. Underlying infrastructure
2. Market topic
3. Share structure and pricing
4. Market initialization
5. Trading
6. Market resolution
7. Settlement
8. Archiving

For each module, enumerates design variants and analyzes trade-offs around decentralization, expressiveness, and manipulation resistance.

### Key Insight
Modern DePMs like Polymarket deviate materially from earlier designs (Truthcoin, Augur v1). Understanding these differences helps identify structural sources of inefficiency.

### Why It Matters for Us
The modular framework provides a mental model for where arbitrage opportunities can arise in each stage of a prediction market's lifecycle.
