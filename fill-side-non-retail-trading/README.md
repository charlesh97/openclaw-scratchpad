# Fill-Side Non-Retail Trading on Polymarket

**Source:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11640

## What it does

An empirical study of non-retail (market maker, arbitrageur, institution) trading on Polymarket. Characterizes the supply-side microstructure at pseudonymous-address scale. Published May 2026 — the most current academic analysis available.

## Key findings

1. **Non-retail traders dominate volume:** Market makers and arbitrageurs account for majority of fill-side activity
2. **Behavioral tiers:** Distinct clusters of professional vs. amateur market makers
3. **Microstructure signatures:** Quote-attribution methods reveal different strategies
4. **NegRisk arbitrage:** Negative risk strategies exist but require sophisticated execution

## Why it matters

Shows that the "easy" arbitrage has been professionalized. But the detailed behavioral taxonomy reveals which strategies still have room for new entrants. Understanding the competitive landscape is critical for strategy selection.

Research paper, no code. Provides competitive intelligence for strategy positioning.
