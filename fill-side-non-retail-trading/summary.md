## In one sentence

The most recent academic study confirming that Polymarket's supply side is dominated by professionals — but reveals pockets where new entrants can still compete.

## Strategic implications

- **Don't compete on speed** for simple arb — you'll lose to HFT bots
- **Focus on combinatorial arb** — still less competitive
- **Target niche categories** (e.g., niche sports, esoteric events) where pro MMs concentrate less
- **Build on the non-retail tier insight** — mid-tier market makers generate reliable returns without fighting for top-of-book alpha
