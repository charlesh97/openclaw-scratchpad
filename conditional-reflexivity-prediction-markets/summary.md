# Summary: Prediction Markets, Conditional Reflexivity, and the Politics of Common Knowledge

**arXiv:2604.24147 | April 2026**

This paper introduces a novel framework: prediction markets as coordination mechanisms, not just forecasting tools. The **Signal Credibility Index (SCI)** combines variance ratio, two-sidedness, and trader concentration to distinguish genuine information revelation from self-fulfilling dynamics.

**Key finding:** The most visible market (Polymarket 2024 election) produced the least accurate forecasts. Highest visibility ≠ highest quality.

**Bottom line:** Valuable conceptual framework for market selection and timing. Not a deployable strategy, but useful for understanding why certain markets behave the way they do.
